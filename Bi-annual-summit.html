Bi-Annual Summit


Our summits are amongst the largest gatherings of young people
in the global public health space in Africa.

A Step towards Growth


Using our CEEC model, we connect young people to accomplished
professionals and change makers in global public health and
encourage networking amongst them.


They are further engaged and empowered with relevant
knowledge and skills and are commissioned to take action in
specific areas towards improving health equity.
