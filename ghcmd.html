Global Health Change Makers Dialogue


This program was launched on the second anniversary of the organisation in 2022. The aim is to encourage robust discussion of key issues that affect young change-makers in the global health space across the continent.

Knowledge Sharing for Global Health


Information sharing is through panellist podcast and video interactions and social media live sessions. Change makers are especially encouraged to share their experience in these sessions which serve as the learning focus.
