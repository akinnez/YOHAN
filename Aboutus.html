Building a Healthier Africa
through Youth Engagement


We are focused on giving young Africans a platform for training,
networking, and exposure to relevant field experience to develop
and deploy high impact solutions to the health challenges of
communities across the continent.






About Us

Healthcare in Africa is perpetually faced with serious challenges which
have continued to widen the health equity gap and have further
hampered development in the continent.

700 million Africans accounting for approximately 50% of the entire
population lack access to quality healthcare with many interventions
not well tailored to the pain points of the end beneficiaries.

Youth Health Action Network (YOHAN Africa) realises the potential to
drive health and development by working with young people who
make up 70% of the continent's population.

We are a youth-led organisation dedicated to giving young people across Africa
the necessary training, networking experience and field exposure to contribute
towards health and development in Africa.

Many of these young people have lived experiences in specific issues concerning
health and well-being and are best positioned to lead the change. We aim to
amplify their voices and encourage commitment to addressing the issues.

  The organisation is fully registered with the Corporate Affairs Commission in
Nigeria (CAC No: 162728) and the Special Control Unit Against Money
Laundering (SCUML) of the Economic and Financial Crimes Commission (EFCC)

What We Do

We give the African youth a platform to implement
innovative ideas, learn, network and we invest in
the continent's development by innovatively
improving the healthcare of her people.

What Moves Us

We are moved by the poor human development index
in the majority of Africa which lack of quality
healthcare is a major contributor. We appreciate that
this can improve if we get involved.

Why We Do It

We believe we have a unique opportunity to
achieve improved healthcare in Africa by working
with young people at the grassroots who have the
ideas, innovation and the passion.

Our Core Values

Integrity:

We maintain integrity in every aspect of running
the organisation. Adhering to proper codes of
conduct and ethics unreservedly.

Sustainability:

Our programs are designed to ensure the impact
made persists and can be replicated, with
proper monitoring and evaluation of programs.

Excellence:

We consistently pursue a culture of excellence in
every aspect of our endeavour. This is
understood and practised by all stakeholders.

Collaboration:

We have demonstrated effective collaboration in
delivering value to our beneficiaries. This has
enabled us to amplify impact and we intend to
continually build on this.

Inclusiveness:

The organization is run by young people of diverse
backgrounds and we entertain no discrimination against
beneficiaries based on race, ethnicity, sexual orientation,
religion or any other factors
