MISSION


We are reimagining Health


To effectively engage the African youth in innovatively promoting holistic
health in communities across the continent through research, health education,
low-cost targeted interventions, advocacy and networking.

VISION


Leading the way to a Healthy Africa


To foster health equity  for human development in Africa

What We Do

We give the African youth a platform to implement
innovative ideas, learn, network and we invest in
the continent's development by innovatively
improving the healthcare of her people.

What Moves Us

We are moved by the poor human development index
in the majority of Africa which lack of quality
healthcare is a major contributor. We appreciate that
this can improve if we get involved.

Why We Do It

We believe we have a unique opportunity to
achieve improved healthcare in Africa by working
with young people at the grassroots who have the
ideas, innovation and the passion.

Our Strategies

Research

We contribute to the scientific body of
knowledge through publications from
data gathered in the course of our
projects, while fully engaging networking
members in research training.

Health Education

We believe that access to health
information is the most effective tool to
achieve behavioural health changes
towards positive health outcomes and
also the cheapest intervention.

Advocacy

We are committed to taking action and being
a voice to attract the much needed attention
of government and other civil society
organisations to prevailing health challenges
facing especially the youth in Africa.

Networking

Our platform provides an opportunity to
leverage a vast network of vibrant,
innovative young people who are
determined to lead sustainable change in
the health of populations in Africa.
